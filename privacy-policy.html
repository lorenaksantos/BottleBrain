🧪 Bottle Brain

Privacy Policy — Last updated April 2026

Bottle Brain is a casual color-sorting puzzle game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the app:

  • Analytics data — anonymised usage events such as which levels you play, how long sessions last, and where you get stuck. This is collected via Mixpanel to help us improve the game. No personally identifiable information is attached to these events.
  • Purchase information — if you purchase the "Remove Ads" subscription, the transaction is processed by Google Play (Android) or the App Store (iOS). We use RevenueCat to verify your subscription status. We never see or store your payment details.
  • Advertising — if you have not purchased "Remove Ads", we display banner ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your name, email, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data Storage

Your game progress (current level, completed levels, purchases) is stored locally on your device using your device's standard storage. We do not upload your progress to any server.

Children's Privacy

Bottle Brain is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Mixpanel — analytics
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google AdMob — advertising

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any analytics data associated with your device at any time by contacting us. Since we do not collect personal identifiers, data is already anonymised by default.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
